Frequently Asked Questions

When will UrbanMD Health Network launch?
We're excited to launch this fall. Join our early access list to be among the first to explore our vast resources designed to help you navigate your health journey at the click of a button!
How can I become a healthcare provider?
Everyone can register their interest through our contact form. When we launch, you'll receive an email notification and healthcare providers will be able to complete their provider registration directly on the platform.
What areas will UrbanMD Health Network serve?
The UrbanMD Health Network is a digital platform designed to serve users across the entire country. It features a variety of resources, including the ability to find a doctor in your local area, as well as access to a wide range of health and wellness products and services.

No matter where you are, UrbanMD is committed to providing you with the tools you need to manage your health effectively.
What role does the UrbanMD Health Network play in healthcare accessibility?
The UrbanMD Health Network plays a vital role in healthcare accessibility by bridging the gap between communities and healthcare providers. It offers a centralized platform where users can easily locate medical professionals and services in their local areas.

By providing a curated database, it streamlines the process of finding reliable healthcare options, making it simpler for individuals to connect with the care they need.
What type of resources does the UrbanMD Health Network provide to promote health and wellness?
The UrbanMD Health Network provides a variety of resources aimed at promoting health and wellness, including:
  • Directory of Healthcare Providers: A curated database of local medical professionals, including doctors, specialists, and clinics.
  • Lifestyle TV Programming: Articles, videos, and expert insights on various health topics.
  • Wellness Products Marketplace: An e-commerce platform offering supplements, fitness equipment, and personal care items.
  • Community Support: Forums and support groups for sharing experiences and connecting with others.
  • Health News and Updates: Regular updates on health initiatives, research, and community events.

Through these resources, the UrbanMD Health Network empowers individuals to take charge of their health and well-being while fostering a supportive community.
What is UrbanMD TV?
UrbanMD TV is a health-centric streaming platform established by Jon Marc Sandifer, a former Programming Executive at BET Networks.

Aimed at promoting healthy living, UrbanMD TV explores diseases impacting urban communities nationwide, offers nutritional guidance, and features expert insights from physicians within the UrbanMD Health Network.
