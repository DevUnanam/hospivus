Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment with a doctor?
You can book an appointment by navigating to the "Appointments" section in the main menu, or by clicking the "Book Appointment" button on the dashboard. You can search for doctors by specialty, location, or availability, and select a convenient time slot.
What insurance plans do you accept?
We accept most major insurance plans, including Blue Cross, Aetna, UnitedHealthcare, Cigna, Medicare, and Medicaid. You can view and update your insurance information in the Settings section. For specific questions about coverage, please contact our billing department.
How do I access my medical records?
Your medical records can be accessed through the "Health Records" section. Here you can view your lab results, doctor's notes, imaging reports, and more. You can also download or share these records with other healthcare providers as needed.
Can I message my doctor directly?
Yes, you can communicate with your healthcare providers through our secure messaging system in the "Messages" section. This allows you to ask questions, request prescription refills, or discuss non-urgent concerns without scheduling an appointment.
